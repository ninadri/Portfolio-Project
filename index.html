Hello my name is Adriana Nino.
I grew up in Harlingen, TX and come from a family of five. My father is a US Marine and my mother works for the state. I moved away from home after graduation to Austin, TX. This had become a dream of mine since I was ten. I couldn't wait to be in the Music Capitol of Texas. When I first arrived in Austin, I worked for HEB. (Fun fact: I grew up right across our local HEB and could see it from my window.) Ever since the pandemic I was looking for a less chaotic atmosphere and was lucky enough to get an offer at a dental office. I've been there for about seven months but still I found myself wanting a challenge. This is why I applied to this bootcamp. Technology terrifies me because I do not understand it. So I figured why not learn. This hasn't been easy but I am willing to try! Because if you're not trying, you're not growing!
